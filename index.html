About Us

Red Mountain is an active participant in the transport and logistics market of USA, providing a full range of services for the transportation of goods of any complexity. Customers appreciate us for their high service, attention to detail and the use of quality standards. We regularly improve our capabilities, introduce new transport schemes and develop existing routes, improve measures to ensure the safety of goods and monitor all the latest trends in transport logistics.

In addition to logistics services, our clients receive high-quality legal support and experience in risk management.

Dry Van

Dry Van services and their consolidation requires a lot of experience to ensure optimal loading of loads and selection of a consignment of goods of appropriate volumes. Our company provides Dry Van services in all directions. Dry Van makes it possible to transport any goods, including consumer goods, for which our company provides the appropriate vehicles.

Expedited

Experienced and attentive Team will perform expedited delivery. Due to the large number of carefully tested and proven logistics schemes, we transport the goods entrusted to us in the optimal time. Expedited service involves the preparation, which protects the client from the negative consequences of various force majeure circumstances

Partial Loads

Partial Loads s popular with many of our customers, including individuals, small companies and large enterprises. Its essence lies in the fact that several small batches of goods are consolidated and transported by one vehicle in one direction.

Drop and Hook

An adopting a drop trailer program will give a chance to shippers and carriers to be independent and work as a team. In this program a driver can come with a tractor to the shipper and pick up a loaded one by exchanging it to the one which was brought.

Reefer

The service efficiently calculates the route taking into account the quality of roads. This allows the shipper no longer spend his own time and energy on telephone calls with drivers. This type of service will be most relevant for the transport of flowers, food (vegetables, fruits, meat products), plants, medicines and frozen convenience foods.

Dispatching

Our Dispatchers simplifies the service by updating you during the transport and they can be on line in a whole time of the service which was the reason of getting a lot of “thanks” from our loyal customers. Our first mission is to make sure that your load is being delivered on time and safely in all the states of the USA.
